## Introduction

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

**MoneyFlow** is a comprehensive Financial management application designed to
help individuals take control of their personal finances. Whether you're
tracking daily expenses, managing budgets, setting savings goals, or analyzing
spending patterns, MoneyFlow provides an intuitive and powerful platform to make
smart Financial decisions.

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

MoneyFlow is your personal finance companion that:

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

### Feature 1: Adding and Managing Transactions

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

**Purpose**: Track all your income and expenses in one place.

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

#### How to Add a Transaction:

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

1. **From Dashboard**:

   - Navigate to the **Dashboard** or **Transactions** page
   - Click the **"+ Add Transaction"** button or **Quick Actions** card

2. **Fill in Transaction Details**:

   - **Description**: Enter what the transaction is for (e.g., "Grocery
     Shopping", "Salary")
   - **Amount**: Enter the amount in rupees (â‚¹)
   - **Category**: Select from predefined categories:

     - Income: Salary
     - Expenses: Food, Rent, Utilities, Entertainment, Other

   - **Payment Method**: Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, Bank Transfer
   - **Date**: Select transaction date (defaults to today)
   - **Attachments** (Optional): Upload receipts or documents

3. **Save Transaction**:

   - Click **"Add Record"** or **"Save"**
   - Transaction appears in your recent transactions list

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

#### Managing Transactions:

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

- **Edit**: Click the edit icon next to any transaction
- **Delete**: Click the trash icon to remove a transaction
- **Filter**: Use date ranges and category filters to find specific transactions
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find transactions by description
- **Sort**: Sort by date, amount, or category

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

#### Split Transactions:

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

For shared expenses:

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

1. Click on a transaction
2. Select **"Split Transaction"**
3. Choose participants
4. Define split method (equal, percentage, or custom amounts)
5. Save split details

## Limitations (Disadvantages)

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

### What MoneyFlow Doesn't Do (Yet):

<!-- @format -->

<!-- @format -->

1. **No Bank Account Integration**

   - Currently requires manual transaction entry
   - Does not automatically sync with bank accounts
   - No automatic transaction imports from banks
   - **Workaround**: Use transaction templates for frequent entries; export bank
     statements and enter in batches

2. **Limited Multi-Currency Support**

   - Primary focus on Indian Rupees (â‚¹)
   - Limited support for multiple currencies
   - No automatic currency conversion
   - **Workaround**: Manually convert currencies before entry; use separate
     categories for foreign transactions

3. **No Investment Portfolio Tracking**

   - Does not track stocks, bonds, or mutual funds
   - No real-time investment valuation
   - Limited investment insights
   - **Workaround**: Use for expense tracking only; maintain separate investment
     tracker

4. **Basic Collaboration Features**

   - Shared expenses limited to splitting bills
   - No multi-user accounts for families
   - Cannot grant others access to your account
   - **Workaround**: Use separate accounts; export and share reports manually

5. **Limited Historical Data Analysis**

   - No multi-year comparative analysis (yet)
   - Historical trends limited to current data
   - Cannot import historical data from other apps
   - **Workaround**: Start using early to build historical data; manually enter
     key past transactions

6. **No Bill Payment Integration**

   - Cannot pay bills directly from the app
   - No integration with payment gateways for bill settlement
   - **Workaround**: Use app for tracking only; pay bills through traditional
     methods

7. **Basic Debt Management**

   - Limited debt tracking features
   - No dedicated debt payoff calculator
   - No credit score tracking
   - **Workaround**: Use custom categories for debt tracking; use external
     calculators for payoff plans

8. **No Tax Calculation**

   - Does not calculate tax liabilities
   - No tax category segregation
   - Limited tax reporting features
   - **Workaround**: Generate reports and share with tax consultant; categorize
     tax-relevant expenses

**Q: Is MoneyFlow free to use?** A: Check the pricing page or contact support

practices. Your data is private and not shared with third parties.

account on any device (phone, tablet, computer). Your data syncs automatically.

deletion option in Profile Settings. Note: This permanently deletes all your
data.

expenses feature allows collaboration without sharing accounts.

export Financial reports as PDF. CSV export may be available in future updates.
